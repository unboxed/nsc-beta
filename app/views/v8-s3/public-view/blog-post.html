Annual call for topics now open

The annual call for new topics in now open. We welcome you to propose a new national screening programme for a condition that is not already considered by the UK NSC.

Published 19 Februrary 2020
From:
Public Health England

The annual call for new topics in now open. We welcome you to propose a new national screening programme for a condition that is not already considered by the UK NSC.

Please download the form and read the guidance at the end of this link to make a proposal.

The deadline for submissions is 4th December 2019.
